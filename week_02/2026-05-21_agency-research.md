**Human-Robot Improv**: Robot Agency 
Can a robot mimic, but not copy, dance motion? Do humans feel more connected to the robot? What counts as improvisation? Can nonhuman motion still feel expressive? Does imperfect or hestitant movement make a robot arm seem more intention or socially alive during dance improvisation?

Pros:
- Incorporate artistic movements
- Likely feasible
Cons:
- Relatively studied

**Robot Imperfect Improv**: Robot Agency
In robot improv, how does the different movement conditions affect perceived agency, emotional connection, trust. Conditions: Perfect, hesitant, corrective, fatigued, searching. Would play different music and robot would improv accordingly. 

Pros: 
- Incorporate artistic movement
Cons: 
- The movement conditions is what makes it a research gap, without that it is relatively covered within the field.
