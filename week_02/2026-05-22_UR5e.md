**Definitions**:
- MoveJ: makes movements that are calculated in the robot arm joint space. Joints are controlled to finish their movements at the same time. This movement type results in a curved path for the tool to follow. 
- MoveL: creates movement that is a direct line from point A to B
- MoveP: creates movement with constant speed between the waypoints.
- MoveCircle: circular movement (go figure) by creating a half circle. You can only ass CircleMove via a MoveP command
