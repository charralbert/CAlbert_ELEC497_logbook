# Observational Behaviors and Emotions to Assess Welfare of Dogs: A Systematic Review
link: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1558787823001491

Stress behaviors: lip-licking, whining, yawning, paw lift, trembling.

Positive behaviors: playing, exploring, affiliative behavior, and tail wagging.

## Stress Responses
Tremble, hard body, hackles raised circling, pacing, paw lift, shake off, raising fur, panting, ...peeing, scratching, lack of appetite, vocalization

## Neutral/interactions with environment
Tail wag, eye contact, accepting treats, physical contact
