# Building Trust in the Age of Human-Machine Interaction: Insights, Challenges, and Future Directions

## Introduction
- "Trust is a foundation for human relationships, facilitating cooperation, collaboration, and social solidarity. Trust in human relationships is generally based on factors like dependability, competence, generosity, and sincerity. Social norms, emotional intelligence, and the power of forecasting others' behaviors help create shared knowledge and mutual respect."
- "Can humans ever trust machines? If they can, how is that trust established, sustained, or dissolved?"

## Human-Human Trust v. Human-Machine Trust
Dependability v Transparency
Generosity v Predictability
Competence v Autonomy and Flexibility
Sincerity v User Experience and Emotional Involvement.
