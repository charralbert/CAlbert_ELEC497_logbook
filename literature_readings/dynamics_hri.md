# The Dynamics of HR Trust Attitude and Behavior - Exploring the effects of anthropomorphism and type of failure
link: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S074756322300359X

## Abstract
two experiments centered around trust dynamics in human-robot interaction: anthropomorphism and type of failure.
- trust attitude is considered to be one of the most important factors for successful collaboration. 
- 1st: participants worked with anthropomorphic or technical robot which made failure
- 2nd: all worked with technical robot.
participants trusted the technical robot more.
For trust attitude, purely decorative anthropomorphic robot design and failures related to the cognitive abilites of robots should be avoided.
