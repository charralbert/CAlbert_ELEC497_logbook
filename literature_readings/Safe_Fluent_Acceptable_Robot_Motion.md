# Safe, Fluent, and Acceptable Motion Generation and Execution for HRI in Manufacturing Environments
link: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.08741

## Abstract 
Robots operating in human environments must not only ensure physical safety but also be understandable, fluent, and acceptable to human partners. Paper investigates motion strategies that combine safety and interaction quality. 
Motion parameters that influence human perception and operator experience. Implement MPS (Model Predictive Control) framework that generates 4 socially-informed robot behaviors.
