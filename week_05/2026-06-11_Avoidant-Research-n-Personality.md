## What Animals Have Anxiety?
link: https://biologyinsights.com/what-animals-have-anxiety-from-pets-to-wildlife/

**How Scientists Define Anixety in Animals**
Indicators: avoidance, excessive vigilance, and repetitive or compulsive actions (pacing, grooming)
Physiologically, anxiety is tracked through changes in the autonomic nervous system, including increased heart rate rapid breathing, and heightened levels of stress.
